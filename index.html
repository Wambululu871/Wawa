A Letter For You

💌
tap to open — you can't close it

I need you to know something. And I need you to actually hear it; not read past it, not brush it off. Hear it.

When I'm around you, the world slows down. Not in the way that feels heavy or stuck; in the way that feels like finally. Like everything that was spinning too fast just....stopped. And I could breathe. I didn't know I was waiting for that until you showed me what it felt like.

You challenge me. You move me out of myself without even trying. You don't let me settle; not because you push me, but because being near you makes me want to be the kind of person who deserves to be near you. That's what you've done without knowing it. You've made me want to be my best. Not for anyone's approval. Just so I can pour every part of that into you; fearlessly, completely, without holding anything back.

I want to tell you something I haven't been able to say this in person to you yet. I want to spend an eternity with you. I know that's a long time. I know we're still at the beginning and everything is still finding its shape. But I mean it with everything I have. Not just as a promise I can't keep; as a truth I can't unfeel.

But here's the thing that scares me most. It's not the uncertainty. It's not the distance between where we are and where I want us to be. It's the thought of temporary. The thought that one day this might just be something that was; and isn't anymore. That thought quite literally aches my soul.

So I built this. Because I wanted something to exist that very clearly said: you mattered before it was official. You were worth showing up for before there was a label. You were already everything; and I already knew.

This is the beginning. And I am so glad it's with you.

— B
