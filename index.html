This letter opens once. Read it slowly.

Hannah.

There is something I have been trying to find the right words for; and I think I finally have them.

Being around you feels like something I cannot fully explain. The world slows down a little. Things that felt heavy feel lighter. And there is this quiet in my chest that I only ever feel when you are near; a feeling that says: this is right. This is where I am supposed to be.

You challenge me without trying to. You make me want to be better; not because you demand it; but because something in me refuses to be less than my best when you are watching. You move me out of comfort zones I did not even know I was hiding in.

I want to spend a long time with you. Not in a desperate way. In the truest way. The kind of way where the thought of this being temporary actually aches.

And I want you to know something important; something I need to say clearly:

You mattered before it was official. You were worth showing up for before there was a label. This page exists because I saw something in you worth building for; worth being intentional about.

So today; on the day the world gets to celebrate you; I want to say this simply:

I am glad you exist. I am glad our paths crossed. And I would very much like to keep choosing this; if you will let me.

— B
